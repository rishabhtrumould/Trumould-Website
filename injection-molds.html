World-Class Mold Engineering from India

The mold is the heart of injection molding. At tru mould, we design and build molds that deliver consistent, repeatable results across millions of cycles. Our in-house mold engineering team uses advanced CAD/CAM software, CNC machining centers, and wire EDM to produce tooling that meets the tightest tolerances.

What sets us apart is our capacity to manage 100+ molds simultaneously — a scale previously only available from Chinese mold shops. Combined with our 20-day Xpress Mold program, we offer speed and scale that's transforming how companies source their tooling.

100+ molds in parallel: Our facility manages over 100 active mold projects simultaneously. This means whether you need one mold or fifty, we have the capacity to deliver without compromising timelines or quality.

Mold Programs

Xpress Mold — 20 Day Delivery

Designed for speed. Xpress Mold uses pre-hardened steel (P20, 718) and streamlined engineering to deliver functional molds in just 20 days. Perfect for market testing, pilot runs, and validating designs before committing to production tooling.

Production Mold — Built for Millions

Engineered for longevity. Production Molds use hardened tool steels (H13, S136, NAK80) with full heat treatment, precision polishing, and advanced cooling systems. Designed for 500,000 to 1,000,000+ cycle mold life.
