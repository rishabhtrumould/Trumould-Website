Production-Ready 3D Models, Engineered for Manufacturing

At tru mould, 3D design isn't just about making something look right — it's about making it manufacturable. Our CAD engineers specialize in creating models that are optimized for injection molding, CNC machining, and 3D printing, ensuring your design transitions seamlessly from screen to production floor.

Every model we produce goes through rigorous DFM (Design for Manufacturability) analysis. We check for wall thickness uniformity, draft angles, undercuts, parting line placement, and material flow — before you commit to tooling. This approach eliminates costly surprises and speeds up your timeline.

DFM-first modeling: Our engineers have designed parts for 500+ production molds. That experience is built into every model we create — catching issues that general-purpose design firms miss.

Design Optimization Capabilities

  • Wall thickness analysis: Uniform wall thickness reduces sink marks, warpage, and cycle time in injection molding.
  • Draft angle optimization: Proper draft ensures clean ejection and longer mold life.
  • Gate and runner design: Optimal gate placement for even material flow and minimal visible gate marks.
  • Snap-fit and living hinge design: Eliminate fasteners and reduce assembly time with integrated features.
  • Multi-part assembly optimization: Reduce part count, simplify assembly, and improve structural integrity.
