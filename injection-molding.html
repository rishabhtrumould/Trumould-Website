Precision Injection Molding, Delivered with Certainty

Injection molding is the backbone of mass manufacturing. At tru mould, we combine advanced tooling technology with rigorous process control to deliver parts that meet your specifications — on time, every time. With a 97.8% on-time delivery rate and over 500 molds delivered, our track record speaks for itself.

We offer two distinct mold programs designed for different stages of your product lifecycle: Xpress Mold for rapid turnaround when speed matters, and Production Mold for high-volume, long-life tooling built with premium steel.

Why India for injection molding? Competitive costs without compromising quality. Our facility operates with the same precision equipment and quality standards as leading manufacturers, with 50% cost savings and significantly lower tariff risk compared to traditional sourcing regions.

Materials We Mold

We process a wide range of engineering thermoplastics, each selected to match your mechanical, thermal, and chemical requirements.

  • Commodity: PP, PE, PS, ABS, SAN
  • Engineering: PC, PA (Nylon 6, 66), POM (Acetal), PBT, PET
  • High-Performance: PEEK, PEI (Ultem), PPS, LCP
  • Elastomers: TPE, TPU, Silicone (LSR)
  • Filled/Reinforced: Glass-filled, carbon-filled, mineral-filled compounds
