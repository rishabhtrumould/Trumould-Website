Precision Parts, Any Material, Any Complexity

From one-off prototypes to production batches of 10,000+, our CNC machining services deliver precision parts in metals and engineering plastics. We operate 3-axis, 4-axis, and 5-axis CNC milling centers alongside CNC lathes, wire EDM, and surface grinders to handle virtually any geometry.

Our sheet metal capabilities complement our machining services, offering laser cutting, bending, welding, and finishing under one roof. This means you get complete assemblies — machined parts, sheet metal enclosures, and hardware — all from a single source.

Single-source advantage: Combining CNC machining with sheet metal fabrication and assembly eliminates coordination between multiple suppliers, reduces lead time, and ensures dimensional consistency across all components.
