Transform Ideas Into Manufacturable Products

Great products start with great design. At tru mould, our concept design services bridge the gap between your initial idea and a fully manufacturable product. Our engineering team works closely with you to understand your requirements, optimize for manufacturing, and deliver production-ready CAD models that minimize tooling costs and lead times.

Unlike traditional design firms that hand off designs without considering production realities, our DFM-first approach means every concept is engineered with injection molding, CNC machining, and 3D printing capabilities in mind from the start. This saves our clients weeks of revision cycles and thousands in tooling rework costs.

Why DFM-first matters: Companies that integrate design for manufacturability from the concept stage see 40-60% fewer tooling revisions and get to market 3-4 weeks faster than those who retrofit designs for production.
